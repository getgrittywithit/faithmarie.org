Psilocybin-Assisted Therapy Shows 67% Remission Rate in Treatment-Resistant PTSD Trial

A Phase 2b randomized controlled trial (n=218) published today in The Lancet Psychiatry reports that two sessions of psilocybin-assisted therapy produced sustained PTSD symptom remission at 12-month follow-up, significantly outperforming prolonged exposure therapy alone.

Confidence: 92% Source: Lancet Psychiatry n = 218 RCT

Gut Microbiome Signature Predicts SSRI Response with 78% Accuracy

Researchers at Stanford identified a 12-species gut microbiome panel that can predict whether a patient will respond to SSRIs before treatment begins. The AI-analyzed meta-study synthesized data from 4 independent cohorts (total n=1,847).

Confidence: 85% Source: Nature Medicine Meta-analysis

Digital CBT App Matches In-Person Therapy for Generalized Anxiety in Largest RCT to Date

A 52-week non-inferiority trial (n=3,200) found that AI-guided cognitive behavioral therapy delivered via smartphone was non-inferior to face-to-face CBT for GAD, with significantly better adherence rates (82% vs 64%).

Confidence: 88% Source: JAMA Psychiatry n = 3,200
