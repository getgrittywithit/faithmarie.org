# Faith Marie Foundation — Family & Close Friends Questionnaire

> These questions are for family members, close friends, and anyone who was personally involved during Faith Marie's life and the time surrounding it. Your perspective matters — you saw things Levi and Lola couldn't see from inside their own grief, and your voice adds depth to Faith Marie's story.
>
> There are no wrong answers. Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with. You can skip anything and come back to it.

---

## About You (3 questions)

1. **What is your name, and what is your relationship to the Moses family?**
   *(e.g., grandmother, aunt, close friend, pastor, etc.)*

2. **How long have you known Levi and Lola? How close were you to the family before Faith Marie?**

3. **Were you involved during the pregnancy, the 21 days, or the time after — or all of it?**
   *(This helps us understand your vantage point)*

---

## When You First Found Out (3 questions)

4. **When and how did you first learn about Faith Marie's heart condition? Who told you, and what was your reaction?**

5. **What went through your mind when you heard the news? What did you feel?**
   *(Fear, sadness, denial, wanting to fix it — whatever was real for you)*

6. **How did you decide to show up for the family? Was there a moment where you knew you needed to be there?**

---

## What You Witnessed (4 questions)

7. **What do you remember about meeting Faith Marie or being at the hospital? Describe a specific moment that stands out.**

8. **What did you see in Levi and Lola during those 21 days? How were they handling it from your perspective?**
   *(Sometimes the people around us see our strength or our pain more clearly than we do)*

9. **Was there a moment during that time that broke your heart — something you witnessed that you'll never forget?**

10. **Was there a moment that gave you hope or made you smile, even in the middle of everything?**

---

## Your Own Grief (3 questions)

11. **How did Faith Marie's death affect you personally? People don't always talk about the grief of extended family and friends — but it's real. What was yours like?**

12. **Did you feel like you had permission to grieve, or did you feel like you had to be strong for Levi and Lola?**

13. **How did you try to support the family after Faith Marie passed? What did you do, and looking back, what do you wish you'd done differently?**

---

## Reflections (3 questions)

14. **What do you wish people understood about what a family goes through when they lose a baby? What did you learn by being close to it?**

15. **How has watching Levi and Lola's journey — from losing Faith Marie to building a foundation in her name — affected the way you think about grief, purpose, or family?**

16. **Is there anything else you want to share — a story, a memory, a detail, or something you want the world to know about Faith Marie or this family?**
   *(This is your open space. Say whatever comes to mind.)*

---

## Form Setup Tips

When you build this in your form tool (Google Forms, JotForm, Typeform, etc.):

- **Make every question optional** — nobody should feel forced to answer something painful
- **Use long-text fields** for all questions except #1 (short text is fine for the name)
- **Add a note at the top** that voice-to-text works great — they can just talk into their phone
- **Add a final question:** *"Can we use your words (with your name or anonymously) on the Faith Marie Foundation website?"* with options: Yes with my name / Yes but anonymously / No
- **Turn off email collection** unless you need it — lower barrier = more responses
- **Send it with a personal text or call first** — don't just drop a link. Let them know why their perspective matters
