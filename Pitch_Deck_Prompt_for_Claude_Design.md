## About the Foundation

The Faith Marie Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (currently filing) that uses autonomous AI agent teams to conduct continuous mental health research — scanning academic papers daily, synthesizing findings into plain language, and publishing free daily digests on grief, PTSD, depression, and anxiety. Alongside the research, the Foundation builds direct support tools: an AI chatbot for real-time empathetic support, a searchable knowledge base, and a resource finder connecting people with therapists and services near them.

**Website:** faithmarie.org (live, MVP in progress)

## The Origin Story (Slide 2 or 3 — this is the emotional anchor)

Faith Marie Moses was the 7th child of Levi and Lola Moses. She was born in 2019 in Texas with a complicated heart condition. She lived for 21 days. The Faith Marie Foundation was born from that gap. Her name now stands for something that helps others through their darkest moments.

## The Problem (make this feel URGENT — this is the #1 takeaway)

- 1 in 5 U.S. adults live with a mental health condition. That's over 50 million people.
- Suicide is the 2nd leading cause of death for ages 10-34.
- The average delay between symptom onset and treatment is **11 years**.
- Groundbreaking mental health research sits locked in academic journals that the public will never read.
- Families dealing with child loss, PTSD, trauma, and depression are left navigating a disconnected, overwhelming landscape with almost no modern tools.
- There is no centralized, AI-powered system that continuously translates mental health research into accessible, actionable support for everyday people.

**The message: People are suffering RIGHT NOW and the current system is failing them.**

## The Solution — Two Pillars

**Pillar 1: AI-Powered Research (the engine)**
Autonomous AI agent teams — each focused on a specific mental health topic — run a full research pipeline daily:
- Scan academic databases and publications for new findings
- Analyze and extract key insights from relevant papers
- Synthesize into plain-language summaries with practical takeaways
- Publish free daily digests via email, website, and social media
- Compile deeper weekly and monthly reports

Initial topic teams: Grief & Child Loss, PTSD & Trauma, Depression, Anxiety (expanding over time).

**Pillar 2: Direct Support Tools (the platform)**
- **AI Support Chatbot** — empathetic, resource-backed conversational AI available 24/7 on faithmarie.org. Not a replacement for therapy, but a first point of contact.
- **Research Knowledge Base** — a searchable public library of all synthesized research, organized by topic and condition, in plain language.
- **Resource Finder** — an intelligent tool that connects people with therapists, support groups, crisis lines, and services based on their location and needs.

## What Makes This Different

- **AI-first from day one** — not digitizing old processes, but building natively with AI agents that work 24/7
- **Research translated daily** — not annual reports or occasional blog posts, but continuous output
- **Everything is free and public** — no paywalls, no premium tiers, no gatekeeping
- **Built by people who lived it** — not academics theorizing about grief, but parents who buried their daughter and decided to build something from the wreckage
